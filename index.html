let me introduce myself,



I’m Queenie Wu, a product + visual designer, ENTP, and a Systems Design Engineering student at the University of Waterloo.

I once thought I loved talking a lot. But then I realized - I love talking because I also love listening. There’s something about a good conversation that leaves me happier than any slice of cake, thus, I’m a designer ignited by collaboration and community. Putting heads together to enable people to do their best is the reason why I design.

Aside from meddling with pixels and palettes, I create art, coach and compete trampoline gymnastics, write about life, and eat everything in my way.

Currently, I’m a design intern at Shopify, making commerce better for everyone. I’m also on the hunt for an exciting summer internship from May to August 2019.
